A Shared Brain for a Drone Swarm

Federated Perpetual Situational Awareness for Covert Swarm Reconnaissance in GPS-Denied Environments

The Challenge: Modern Missions are Blind

Missions in complex environments face critical vulnerabilities.

GPS-Denied Zones

Drones can't navigate in tunnels, dense forests, or urban canyons where satellite signals are blocked.

High-Risk Operations

Sending a single, expensive drone is risky. If it fails, the mission is over.

Vulnerable Communication

Centralized control is a single point of failure, and streaming video is easy to detect and jam.

Our Solution: A Swarm That Learns Together

A decentralized team of mini-drones that build a collective intelligence in real-time.

1. SEE: Bat-Like Vision

Each drone uses an Event Camera, allowing it to navigate perfectly in the dark and at high speeds without motion blur.

2. SPOT: Onboard AI

A lightweight Mini-AI on each drone analyzes its surroundings, spotting potential threats and anomalies on its own.

3. LEARN: Individual Insight

When a drone sees a new threat, its Mini-AI learns from the observation, updating its own understanding of the environment.

4. SHARE: The Smart Tip

The drone shares this new learning—a tiny, secure data packet—with its neighbors. This is Federated Learning.

5. AGGREGATE: The Shared Brain

The entire team combines their individual learnings to create a new, smarter, and more robust Shared Brain for the whole swarm.

A Transformative Leap in Capability

RESILIENT

No central leader means no single point of failure. The loss of several drones won't stop the mission.

STEALTHY

Sharing tiny "learning" packets has a 1000x smaller communication footprint than video, making it nearly undetectable.

ADAPTIVE

The swarm gets smarter during the mission, learning to identify new and camouflaged threats as a team.
